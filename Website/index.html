Team Associated Rc10

Team Associateds Rc10 was the beginning of an era of 2wd racing that is still treasured to this day. This series of going from nitro to electric vehichles changed the name of racing. In 1993 the very first model was realeased.

Team Associated also has a series of other ehichles offered for the use of the track and plain outdoor use.The RC10 revolutionized the world of off-road racing and the RC hobby as a whole. To racers and collectors alike, it’s one of the most important cars in the history of RC. The RC10 established the benchmark for off-road racing performance, and shaped the design of every car that followed it. Much has been said about its wild success, track prowess, and its modern legacy, and it’s clear that without the RC10, our hobby would be very different today. Over the course of 16 years, the RC10 won eight national championships, four world titles, and the hearts of off-roaders around the world.
